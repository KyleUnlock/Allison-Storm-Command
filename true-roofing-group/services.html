Roof Repair

A leak doesn’t wait, and neither do we. We find the real source — not just the wet spot — and fix it to last.

Too many “repairs” are a dab of sealant that fails by the next storm. We trace water to its origin, show you photos of the cause, and repair it so the same leak doesn’t come back on your dime.
