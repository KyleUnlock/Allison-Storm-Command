Our story

An owner-led roofer, on purpose.

True Roofing Group was built to be the company homeowners wish they’d called first.

Founded on a simple standard

True Roofing Group started with a frustration Kris Juric heard on job site after job site: homeowners who’d been burned by a fast-talking contractor, a roof that leaked within a year, and a warranty that turned out to be worthless.

So Kris built the opposite. A roofing company where the owner’s name means something, where crews are trained and accountable, and where the paperwork you’re handed at the end is a warranty you can actually cash. No storm-chasing, no pressure, no cut corners hidden under new shingles.

Today that standard runs through every job — a $600 repair gets the same honesty and the same clean job site as a full replacement.
