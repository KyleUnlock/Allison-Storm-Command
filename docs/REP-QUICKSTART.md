# Rep Quickstart — Allison Storm Command

For Allison Roofing field reps. This is a **staged draft for review** — it has
not been sent. Your name and passcode are handed to you separately; they are
never written down here.

---

## The deal, in one sentence

UnlockAI earns **20% of the profit** on a job you close (the money collected
minus the direct job costs) — never a cut of revenue — so the fee only exists
when Allison actually makes money.

---

## 1. Log in

1. Go to **`/login`** and sign in with the name + passcode Allison gave you.
2. You land on **`/rep`**, your personal console. You see **only your own
   leads** — never anyone else's, and never the operator board.

## 2. Claim and work leads

- Your assigned leads show on `/rep`. Open one to see the homeowner's details,
  the storm context, and its history.
- Work a lead by moving it through the stages (next section) and logging what
  happened after each touch.

## 3. Reaching out — the outreach composer

The composer helps you prepare a call or text, but **it never dials or texts
anyone for you**. There is no auto-dialer, no auto-SMS, and no AI voice. You are
always the one who picks up the phone.

- The composer only lets you log a **call** or **sms** attempt when the
  compliance light is **green**:
  - **Call:** allowed only when the number passed a Do-Not-Call scrub
    (`callable`). If it hasn't, the call is blocked — do not dial it.
  - **Text:** allowed only when the homeowner gave SMS consent and has **not**
    opted out. A `STOP`/opt-out number can never be texted.
- A blocked attempt is recorded with its reason. A permitted attempt is
  recorded and stamps your first-touch time. **After** the composer clears you,
  you dial or text by hand. If the light is red, the answer is: don't contact
  that way.

## 4. Log notes

After every touch, add a note on the lead. Notes are append-only and
time-stamped — they build the lead's timeline and protect the attribution that
the deal depends on.

## 5. Move stages: new → … → won → completed

Advance the lead as it progresses through the pipeline:

**new → contacted → inspection → inspected → quoted → negotiation → won →
production → completed**

- Move a lead to **won** when the homeowner signs. That's the point the
  20%-of-profit fee is calculated against the real collected/cost numbers.
- Keep going to **production** and **completed** as the job is built and closed
  out.

---

## Quick rules

- You only ever see and touch **your own** leads.
- Green light before you contact — DNC for calls, consent (no opt-out) for
  texts. When in doubt, don't.
- Log every touch. The note timeline is what keeps a Won lead credited to you.
